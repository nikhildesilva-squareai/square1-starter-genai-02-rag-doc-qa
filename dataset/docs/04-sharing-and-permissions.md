# Sharing and Permissions

Share a note or notebook with the **Share** button. You can invite people by
email and assign one of three permission roles:

- **Viewer** — can read but not edit.
- **Commenter** — can read and leave comments.
- **Editor** — can read, edit, and re-share.

Only Pro and Team plans can share entire notebooks; the Free plan can share
individual notes by read-only link. Share links can be set to expire after a
chosen number of days, and an admin can revoke any link from the audit log.
