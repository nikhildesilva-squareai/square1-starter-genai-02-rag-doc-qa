# Pricing and Plans

Nimbus Notes has three plans:

- **Free** — 1 user, 3 notebooks, 100 MB of attachments. $0/month.
- **Pro** — unlimited notebooks, 10 GB of attachments, version history.
  $8 per user per month, billed monthly, or $80 per user per year.
- **Team** — everything in Pro plus shared spaces, admin roles, and SSO.
  $14 per user per month.

Annual billing saves roughly 17% versus monthly. You can upgrade or downgrade at
any time from **Settings → Billing**; downgrades take effect at the end of the
current billing period. We accept credit card and PayPal.
